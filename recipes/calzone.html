Calzone

Description

This calzone recipe can be split in half into 2 regular calzones. You can fill them with anything!

Ingredients

    1 and 1/4 cups water, 1 teaspoon, 3 cups bread flour, 1 teaspoon dry milk powder, 1 and 1/2 tablespoons white sugar, 2 teaspoons active dry yeast, 3/4 cup pizza sauce, 4 ounces chopped pepperoni sausage, 1 and 1/4 cups shredded, 2 tablespoons melted butter
